Telaga Waja Rafting

Two hours Telaga waja rafting adventure at Telaga Waja river. Start point in Muncan village, northeast of Bali and one of longest river in Bali start running to south of Bali to southern slop of mount Abang. Best spot for rafting adventure and quite challenging your adrenaline.
