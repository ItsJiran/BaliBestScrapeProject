About North Bali Tours

North Bali Tours is one of popular Bali full day tour package which is special for explore the most popular tourist attractions in the northern part of Bali island. With this tour you will visit Ulun Danu Temple, Mengwi Temple, Gitgit Waterfall, Banjar hot spring, Wanagiri Hidden Hill and Luwak coffee plantation.

Bali North Tour has been designed perfectly, however we are happy to change with other tourist attractions based on guest request or budget. Please check our tour itinerary as below and please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour

#1. Visit Taman Ayun Temple

#2.Visit Ulun Danu Temple

#3. Visit Git Git Waterfall

#4. Lunch at local restaurant and then visit Banjar Hot Spring

#5. Visit Wanagiri Hidden Hill

#6. Visit Luwak Coffee Plantations
