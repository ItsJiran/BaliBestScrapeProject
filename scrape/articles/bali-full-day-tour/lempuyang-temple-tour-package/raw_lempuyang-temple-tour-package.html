Lempuyang Temple Tour Package

Lempuyang temple tour package or East Bali Tour is popular Bali tour which is special to visit the main gate of Lempuyang temple located in the slopes of Mount Lempuyang, Karangasem regency, east part of Bali island. Lempuyang temple or you can call it Pura Penataran Agung Lempuyang is one of six holy temple which is highly respected and safeguarded on Bali island.

Interesting places will be visiting during this tour

#1. Lempuyang temple

#2. Tirta Gangga beautiful water garden

#3. Taman Ujung Water Palace

#4. Candidasa Beach

#5. Goa Lawah Temple or Bat Cave
