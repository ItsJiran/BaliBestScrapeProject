Kintamani Tour

Kintamani Tour is an exciting Bali Full Day tour packages and one of popular Bali Tour that you can do when on vacation on the island of Bali. The tour will take you to visit amazing views of Mount and Lake Batur from the top of Penelokan village, Kintamani.

The Tour itinerary has been designed with you in mind, however we are more than happy if you would like to change with other tourist attractions based on your request. Please check our tour itinerary as below and please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.

Interesting places will be visited during this Tour

1.Visit Batubulan village

Pick up at hotel at 08.00 a.m and then visit Batubulan village located in west of Gianyar regency.  Batubulan village has been famous all over the world as create the spectacular Balinese dance performance that is Barong and Kris dance.

Barong and kris dance is Balinese traditional dance which is the story correlated with the struggle between the benevolence and bad spirit. This story taken from Mahabharata Epic, especially the period of Nakula and Sahadewa.

2.Visit Celuk Village

hen Visit Celuk village to see incredible gold and silver handicraft, located in sub district of Sukawati, Gianyar regency. Right place to see Balinese professional, artistic and skillful of design development related to the Gold and silver crafting.

The productions have penetrated to the local, nation and international market. The type of gold and silver craft has been produced in this countryside are covering various of rings, bangles, chokers, earrings, mothers of pearls, Tusuk konde, broach and others types.

3.Visit Tirta Empul Temple

Tirta Empul temple is Hindus temple located in Manukaya village. Tirta Empul well known with holy spring water which is believed to have magical powers. a popular place to visit in Bali island by both domestic and foreign tourist.

4.Visit Luwak Coffee plantation

After visit Tirta Empul Temple continue to visit Luwak Coffee plantation. Here you can see and taste many kinds of coffee, and one of the coffee will be serve is Luwak coffee. Luwak coffee is seeds taken from civet droppings and then undergoes fermentation naturally then issued in the form of coffee beans and believed to have different flavors that have a higher quality than arabica coffee.

4.Visit Kintamani village

Visit Kintamani village to see beautiful panorama of Batur Volcano and Batur lake from Penelokan countryside. Batur volcano is the second highest volcano in Bali, it is about 1700 meters above sea level. After that we will drive you to local restaurant for having lunch and pampered by volcano and Batur lake view.

5.Visit Tegalalang Rice Terrace

Visit Tegalalang rice terrace is our last destination on this tour. This place located in Tegalalang countryside, north of Ubud. Only 20 minutes drive from Ubud Area. Tegalalang rice terrace is popular tourist attraction which offer beautiful scenery of terraced of rice fields.
