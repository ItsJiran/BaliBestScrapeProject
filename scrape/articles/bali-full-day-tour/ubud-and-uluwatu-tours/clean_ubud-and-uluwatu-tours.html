Ubud and Uluwatu Tours

Ubud and Uluwatu Tours is one of our exciting full day tour package which is duration 10 – 12 hours to visit places of interest in Ubud village and visit Uluwatu Temple. Places of interest will be visited in Ubud such as visit Ubud Monkey Forest, Batubulan village to see Barong Dance and visit Batik hand making, Celuk village to see Silver handicraft and Batik making. Visit Mas Village to see Balinese Painting which is popular on the Bali island with  central of Art.

And then continue to visit Uluwatu temple which is one of best attraction on the Bali island which is offer beautiful panorama with a spectacular sunset and beautiful view of Indian ocean from top of cliff bank. And then  we will invite you to watching Kecak dance performance. Kecak dance is a mass dance performance which describes the role of “ puppet story” namely Rama and Sita. And then dinner at Jimbaran bay, this place is special for grill seafood dinner with beach view. Dinner in Jimbaran bay is our last programs of this tour and then back to hotel.
