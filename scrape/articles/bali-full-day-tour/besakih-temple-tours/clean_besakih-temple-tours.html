Besakih Temple Tours

Besakih Temple Tours is one of the most popular Bali full day tour packages which duration 10 – 12 hours tour trip. Visit Besakih Temple is our main itinerary on this tour. This temple well known with “mother temple” and the biggest temple in the island of Bali.

Beside that this tour will take you to visit others interesting places such as: visit Celuk villages to see Gold and silver smith, visit mas village to see Art painting, visit Kintamani village, visit luwak coffee plantations and visit Tegalalang rice terrace.

The tour has been designed based on our experience and our guest recommendation, however we can replace it if you want to change with other tourist attractions. Please have a look our tour itinerary as below and please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.
