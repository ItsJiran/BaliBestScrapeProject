Ubud and Tanah Lot Tours

Ubud and Tanah Lot Tour is an exciting Bali tour, and one of popular Bali Full Day Tour package with duration 10 – 12 hours to visit Ubud village, Tanah Lot temple and other interesting places. Visit Ubud village and Tanah Lot temple is our main destination on this tour package. Which is Ubud village popular by its beautiful landscape and famous as art village. And then Tanah Lot temple is favorite sight in Bali island, and one of Sad Kahyangan temple or called one of six temples of the holy temple in Bali. Which is very sacred by Hindus in Bali as the protector of Bali Island from negative influence.
