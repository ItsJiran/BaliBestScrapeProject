Interesting places will be visited during this tour

#1. Visit Taman Ayun Temple

Taman Ayun Temple is royal temple located in Mengwi village. Taman Ayun temple founded by royal Mengwi that consists of three parts on it: the outer, middle and inner parts, each part has a different function. Third part of the temple there are additional exterior surrounded by a large pond so that the existence of the temple is look beautiful.

#2. Visit Ulun Danu Temple

Visit Ulun Danu Temple located in Candi Kuning countryside, Baturiti sub district and Tabanan regency. Ulun Danu Temple is Balinese Hindu temple set on lakeside of Beratan with beautiful view of lake and serrounding by hills. This temple is a place of worship to Sang Hyang Dewi Danu which is dedicated as the giver of the lives of all beings on this earth.

#3. Candi Kuning Fruits Market

Candi kuning Fruits Market or Candi Kuning market is Balinese traditional fruits  and vegetable market situated on the main way of Bedugul – Singaraja.  Here you can find fresh fruits and vegetable due to supplied from the local agriculture field surrounding area.

#4 . Visit Bali Luwak coffee plantation

Here you can see and taste many kinds of coffee, and one of the coffee will be serve is Luwak coffee. Luwak coffee is seeds taken from civet droppings and then undergoes fermentation naturally then issued in the form of coffee beans and believed to have different flavors that have a higher quality than arabica coffee.

#5. Visit Alas Kedaton Monkey forest

Alas Kedaton Monkey Forest is small rain forest dwelt by the group of monkey’s located in Kukuh countryside, Marga sub district, Tabanan regency. In this place there is a temple called Alas Kedaton temple environment by frest air, calm atmosphere. 

#6. Visit Tanah Lot Temple

Tanah Lot Temple is favorite sight on the island of Bali, located on the beach and set on the rock if the water tide will appear floating temple. Tanah Lot Temple is a Sad Kahyangan temple or called one of six temples of the holy temple in Bali. Which is very sacred by Hindus in Bali as the protector of Bali island from negative influence. Visit Tanah Lot temple is our last itinerary on this tour and then back to hotel.
