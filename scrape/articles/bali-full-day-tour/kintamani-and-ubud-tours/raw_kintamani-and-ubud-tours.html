Kintamani and Ubud Tours

Kintamani and Ubud Tours is Bali Full Day Tours package which is one of popular tour package in Bali island with duration 10 – 12 hours to visit places of interest in Kintamani village and Ubud village. Includes visiting Batubulan village to see Barong dance performance, and then visit Celuk village to see gold and silver crafting, ahead to visit Tirta Empul temple which is famous for its holy water for Hindu Balinese purification, and then continue to visit Bali coffee plantation. After that, we will take you to visit Kintamani village to see beautiful view of mount Batur and the lake. Continue to visit Tegalalang rice terrace to see beautiful view of rice terrace in Tegalalang village and then back to hotel.

This tour itinerary has been designed with you in the mind, however we can change it by visit other tourist spots based on guest request if one of our programs may you have visited. Our tour guide ready to recommend best places to visit in Bali outside the program that we offer. During the trip, our car is equipped with air conditioning and  driver has been trained so your trip feel comfortable.
