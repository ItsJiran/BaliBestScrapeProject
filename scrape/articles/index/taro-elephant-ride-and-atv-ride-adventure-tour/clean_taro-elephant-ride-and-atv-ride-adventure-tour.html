Taro Elephant Ride and ATV Ride Adventure Tour

Taro Elephant Ride and ATV Ride Adventure Tour is one of the most popular things to do in the island of Bali. The tour will take you to enjoy 30 minutes elephant ride at Taro Forest. On this place you can watch Elephants playful Bathing ritual, touch and hand feed Elephants, take photo with elephants, witness live aducational shows and elephants painting. After that our driver will drive you to Singapadu village for 2 hours atv ride, enjoy the panorama of the countryside, rice field views.
