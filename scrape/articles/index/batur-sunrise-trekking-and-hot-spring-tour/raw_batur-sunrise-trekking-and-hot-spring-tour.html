Batur Sunrise Trekking and Hot Spring Tour

One of the most popular Bali adventure we created for those of you who are would like to do Trekking in Bali. Batur Sunrise Trekking and Hot Spring Tour is a combination Bali tour we created to experience 2 activities in one day trip. With this tour “Batur trekking and natural hot spring tour“, you will see beautiful view of sunrise from the summit of Mount Batur and enjoy the panoramic lake batur closer and soak in natural hot spring.

Book your trip now and pay upon pick up at yur hotel. Feel free to contact us for best arrangement

Tour Itinerary:

  • 01:30 – Pick up at your hotel
  • 03:30 – Arrive at starting point and then having breakfast
  • 03:30 – Small briefing by professional trekking guide
  • 04:00 – Start for climb to the crater summit
  • 06:00 – Arrive at crater summit and then enjoy the beautiful sunrise views
  • 07:00 – Continue your trek around the entire crater of mount batur and then back to finish point
  • 08:00 – Arrive at finish point
  • 08:30 – Visit Batur Natural Hot spring. Spend  your time for 2 hours and swim at Natural hot spring and enjoy the panoramic beauty of lake Batur a little bit closer.
  • 11:00 – Finish and then back to hotel
