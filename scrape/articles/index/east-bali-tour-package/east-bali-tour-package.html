East Bali Tour Package

East Bali Tour Package

East Bali Tour Package is one of best way to explore interesting places in east part of Bali island. The tour duration approximately 10 – 12 hours tour trip.  The tour start at 08:00 am from your hotel and then go to Kelungkung for visit Kertha Gosa Court of justice. After that continue to visit Goa Lawah Temple or you can call it Bat Cave Temple located on Jl. Goa Lawah, Kelungkung regency.

Then visit Tenganan Traditional village located in Manggis, Karangasem Regency well known as Bali Age or Bali Ancient Villages. After that continue to visit Tirta Gangga Water Garden or you can call it Royal water Garden which is was built in 1946 during the raign of the late of Karangasem kindom.  After that visit Taman Ujung Sukasada and then back to hotel.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour

#1. Kertha Gosa Court of justice

#2. Goa Lawah Temple or Bat Cave Temple

#3. Tenganan Traditional Village

#4. Tirta Gangga Water Garden

#5. Taman Ujung Sukasada

Tour Itinerary:

  • 08:00 – Pick up at hotel
  • 09:00 – Visit Kertha Gosa Court of justice
  • 10:30 – Visit Goa Lawah Temple or Bat Cave Temple
  • 12:00 – Lunch at local restaurant
  • 13:00 – Visit Tenganan Traditional Village
  • 15:00 – Visit Tirta Gangga Water Garden
  • 16:00 – Visit Taman Ujung Sukasada
  • 16:30 – Back to Hotel
