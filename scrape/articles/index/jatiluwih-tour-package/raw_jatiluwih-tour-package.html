Jatiluwih tour package

Jatiluwih tour package is a Bali full day tour which duration 10 – 12 hours tour trip which special to visit Jatiluwih rice terraces as wellknown as UNISCO world heritage located in Tabanan regency. Other interesting places will be visited such as: Visit Mengwi Temple, Luwak coffee plantation, Ulun Danu temple, Alas Kedaton monkey forest and Tanah Lot temple. This tour package has been designed based on guest request in general, however we will be happy to change it basedon customer request if one of our tour itinerary has been visited.

Places of interest will be visited:

#1 . Taman Ayun Temple

Visit Taman Ayun Temple is our first stop on this trip. Taman Ayun temple is royal temple which is located in Mengwi village. Taman Ayun temple founded by royal royal Mengwi.

#2. Luwak Coffee plantation

On this place you can see and taste many kinds of coffee, also they will serve Luwak coffee which is one of best coffee in Bali. Luwak Coffee is seeds taken from civet droppings and then undergoes fermentation naturally then issued in the form of coffee beans and believed have different flavors that have a higher quality than arabica coffee.

#3. Ulun Danu Temple

Ulun Danu Temple is Balinese Hindu Temple located in Candi Kuning Village. Set on lake side of Beratan and surrounding by beautiful lake and hills. This temple is a place of worship to Sang Hyang Dewi Danu which is dedicated as the giver of the lives of all beings on this earth.

#4. Jatiluwih Rice Terrace

Jatiluwih rice terrace is UNISCO world heritage located in Jatiluwih village. On this place you can spend your time just few hours to walk and see the beauty of rice terrace view which is will accompanyed by professional Bali tour guide. Read more…

#5. Tanah Lot Temple

Visit Tanah Lot is our last trip on this itinerary. Tanah Lot Temple set on the rock and one of popular destination in Bali especially at sunset time. Read more…
