Sekumpul Waterfall Trekking Package

Sekumpul Waterfall Trekking Package is Combination Bali full day tour  and one of popular Bali trekking with duration 10 – 12 hours which is Sekumpul waterfall trekking is our main itinerary. This trekking will take 3 hours to explore the beauty of waterfall and countryside.

And then enjoy you lunch at local restaurant pampered by beautiful view of Tamblingan dan Buyan lake. After that continue to visit Ulun Danu Temple located in Beratan lake, which is this temple set on the lake and dedicated to the Ida Batari Dewi Ulun Danu or Godness of the lake. Then visit Luwak coffee plantation which is on this place you will see how to make a coffee with Balinese traditional prosses after that they will serve you a lot of type of coffee product. And then visit Mengwi temple or royal king temple located in Mengwi countryside and then back to hotel.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour

#1. Sekumpul Waterfall Trekking

#2. Visit Ulun Danu temple

#3. Luwak Coffee plantation

#4. Taman Ayun Temple or Royal king
