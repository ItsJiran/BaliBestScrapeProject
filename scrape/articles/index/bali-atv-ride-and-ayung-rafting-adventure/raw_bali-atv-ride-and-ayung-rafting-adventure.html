Bali ATV Ride and Ayung Rafting Adventure

Bali ATV Ride and Ayung Rafting Adventure is a popular Bali adventure tour that you can do while in the island of Bali. The tour start at 08:00 am from your hotel and then our driver will take you to Ayung Rafting to enjoy 2 hours Ayung Rafting adventure and finish at 12:00 noon and then enjoy lunch which is will be serve after Bali Rafting activities. And then our driver will take you to the Singapadu village to enjoy 2 hours ATV ride. This tour has been designed based on our experience in the tourist field, however we are more than happy to change it if you want to change it with other activities. Please have a look our best price and tour itinerary as below and please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.
