Tourist interesting places will be visited

Day 1: Ubud and Kintamani Volcano Tour

#1 visit Batubulan village

Pick up at Hotel at 08.00 a.m and then our driver will you to visit Batubulan village located in the west part of Gianyar regency. Batubulan village has been famous all over the world as create the spectacular Balinese dance performance or known as Barong and kris dance. Barong and Kris dance is Balinese traditional dance which is the story correlated with the struggle between the benevolence and bad spirit. This story taken from Mahabharata Epic, especially the period of Nakula and Sahadewa. The performance start at 09.30 am and finish at 10.30 am.

#2 Visit Celuk village

Visit Celuk village to see incredible gold and silver handicraft, located in sub district of Sukawati, Gianyar regency. Best place to see Balinese professional artistic and skillful of design development related to the Gold and silver crafting.

The productions have penetrated to the local, nation and international  market. You can buy for present or for your own. The type of gold and silver craft has been produced in this countryside are covering various of rings, bangles, chokers, earrings, mothers of pearls, Tusuk konde, broach and others types.

#3 Visit Mas village

Mas village famous with wood carving center which is most of the local people creatively create the beautiful handicraft. And one villages in Bali fundamentally identified as an artistic countryside by focusing in artistic of wood carving.

#4 Visit Tirta Empul Temple

Visit Tirta Empul Temple Located in Manukaya village, Gianyar Regency. Tirta Empul temple well known with holy spring water which is believed to have magical powers. You can do a self-purification ritual in this holy water to remove the negative influences that you have in your body and mind.

#5 Visit Luwak Coffee Plantation

Visit Coffee Plantation on the way to Kintamani Volcano.

Visit Luwak Coffee Plantation. On the way to Kintamani Volcano we will stop a moment to visit Bali Coffee plantation. Here you can see how coffee is made in the traditional way, and get to know the various kinds of coffee. One of the most popular today is Luwak coffee.

#6 Visit Kintamani Volcano

Visit Kintamani Village to see beautiful panorama of Batur Volcano and Batur lake. Number two of highest volcano in Bali, it is about 1700 meters above sea level.

#7 Visit Banjar Hot Spring water

Banjar Hot Spring is natural water that emerges from the ground to the surface who is containing brimstone substances. Banjar Hot Spring existing around a hundred years and was built in the Japanese colonial era and as bathing place. A lot of people believe that the water can cure some diseases such as ringworm and other skin diseases.

Day 2: North Bali Tour

#1 Watching Dolphins in Lovina Beach

Wake up early morning at 5.30 a.m and then going to Lovina beach for watching dolphin in the sea use traditional boat. At 8.00 a.m back to hotel to have breakfast and then at 8.30 a.m check out from hotel and start for next trip.

#2 Visit Git git waterfall

Gitgit waterfall is one of best place to visit in north of Bali located in Gitgit countryside, Sukasada Sub district and Buleleng regency. Its height reaches 35 meters and is one of the highest waterfalls on the island of Bali. you have to walk through clove and coffee plantations, it will take around 20-30 minutes to reach the location. When you arrive at the location you will be spoiled by the natural atmosphere surrounded by tropical forests that are protected by the local community.

#3 Visit Ulun Danu Temple

Visit Ulun Danu Temple located in Candi Kuning countryside, Baturiti sub district and Tabanan regency. Ulun Danu Temple is Balinese Hindu Temple set on the lakeside.

#4 Visit Jatiluwih Rice Terrace

Jatiluwih rice terrace is biggest rice terrace in Bali located in Tabanan regency, and one of famous tourist destination in the west part of Bali which is offered by the breathtaking view of rice terrace unfolding from the foot of mountain.

Jatiluwih Rice Terrace has been well appointed as one of UNESCO World Heritages due this place has been able to maintain the local culture heritage, that is SUBAK. SUBAK is form water irrigation system managed traditionally

#5 Visit Tanah Lot Temple

Tanah Lot Temple is favorite sight on the island of Bali, located on the beach and set on the rock if the water tide will appear floating temple. Tanah Lot Temple is a Sad Kahyangan temple or called one of six temples of the holy temple in Bali. Which is very sacred by Hindus in Bali as the protector of Bali Island from negative influence.
