Mount Batur Sunrise Trekking Packages

Mount Batur Sunrise Trekking Packages is one of exciting Bali trekking tours to climb to the summit of Mt. Batur at early morning. Mount Batur is one of the active volcanoes in Indonesia which reaches 1.717 meters above sea level and the third highest peak on Bali island.

Batur Sunrise Trekking Package starting at 3.30 am from the starting point and it takes 2 hours to get to the top of mount Batur. Take rest on the top while waiting the sun rises at 6 am, see the majestic of neighbouring mount Abang hovering above the clouds, see the top of Mount Agung which is the biggest mount on the Bali island.

Tour itinerary:

  • 01:30 – Pick up at the hotel
  • 03:00 – Arrive at start point
  • 03:15 – 04:00 Enjoy your breakfast and then quick briefing by professional trekking guide
  • 04:00 – 06:00 Start for climb to the crater summit
  • 06:15 – 06:45 Arrival at the top of mount Batur where we soak up the spectacular sunrise and you can boiled eggs in volcanic steam.
  • 07:00 – 07:15 Continue to trek around  the entire crater
  • 08:00 – 08:15 Back to finish point
  • 09:30 – 10:00 Arrive at finish point and then back to hotel
