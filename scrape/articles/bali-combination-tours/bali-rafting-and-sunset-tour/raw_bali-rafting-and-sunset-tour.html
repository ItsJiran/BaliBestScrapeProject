Bali Rafting and Sunset Tour

Bali Rafting and Sunset Tour is an exciting Bali combination tour package we created to enjoy 2 hours rafting adventure at Ayung river and visit Tanah Lot Temple to see amazing sunset. The tour start at 08:00 am from your hotel and then our driver will take you to Ayung River for 2 hours rafting adventure. Finish at 12:00 and then our driver will take you visit the most popular tourist such as Mengwi Temple, Luwak coffee plantation and Tanah Lot Temple.

Bali Ayung Rafting and Sunset tour itinerary has been designed based on our experience on the field, however we can replace by visit other Bali sightseeing based on your request. Please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.

Places of interest will be visited during this tour

#1. Rafting activities

#2, Mengwi Royal Temple or Taman Ayun Temple

#3. Alas Kedaton Monkey Forest

#4. Tanah Lot Temple

Tour Itinerary:

  • 08:00 – Pick up at the hotel
  • 09:30 – Arrive at starting point
  • 10:00 – Start Rafting Adventure
  • 12:00 – Buffet Lunch
  • 13:00 – Visit Taman Ayun Temple
  • 15:00 – Visit Luwak Coffee plantation
  • 16:00 – Visit Tanah Lot Temple
  • 18:00 – Back to hotel
