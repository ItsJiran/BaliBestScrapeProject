Bali Zoo and Uluwatu Tours

Bali Zoo and Uluwatu Tours is one of our best Bali combination tour package which is  duration of tour is 10 – 12 hours. With this tour, we will visit Bali Zoo located on Jalan Singapadu, Gianyar regency and one of lush tropical garden with area 12 hectares. And then our driver will take to the south part of Bali to visit Uluwatu Temple located in Pecatu Village, south part of Bali which is Built on top of cliff bank and then at 6.00 pm we will watching Kecak Fire Dance with duration 1 hour located on the left side of Uluwatu Temple and then back to hotel.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour

#1. Bali Zoo

#2. Luwak Coffee Plantations

#3. Uluwatu Temple

#4. Kecak & Fire Dance
