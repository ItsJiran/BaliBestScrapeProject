Bali Rafting and Besakih Tours

Bali Rafting and Besakih Tours is one of an exciting Bali combination tour package we created to join with us for 3 hours Rafting adventure at Telaga Waja river. Telaga Waja River is one of the longest river in the eastern part of Bali. Finish at 1pm and then our driver will take you to visit Besakih Temple which is well known as Mothers Temple and known as biggest temple in the island of Bali. After that continue to visit Kintamani village to see beautiful view of Mount Batur and Batur lake. Then visit Luwak coffee plantation to see how to make coffee with Bali traditional process, find the best coffee quality . Then visit Tegalalang Rice Terrace located in Tegalalang countryside, our last tourist attraction on this tour

The tour itinerary has been designed based on our experience in the field and our guest recommendation, however we can replace by visit other Bali sightseeing based on your budget. Please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.
