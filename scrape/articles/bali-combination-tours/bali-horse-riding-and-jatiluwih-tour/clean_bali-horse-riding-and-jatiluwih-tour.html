Bali Horse Riding and Jatiluwih Tour

Horse Riding and Jatiluwih Tour is one of the most popular Bali Combination tour package that you can do while in Bali island. This tour will take you to enjoy 1 hours horse riding adventure and then our driver will take you to visit popular tourist attractions such as visit Tanah Lot temple, visit Jatiluwih Rice Terrace, Visit Ulun Danu Temple and Visit Luwak Coffee plantation.

This tour has been designed with customer in mind, however we can replace by visit other Bali sightseeing based on guest request if one of our itinerary have been visited before. Our tour guide ready to provide recommendations best place to visit in Bali outside with our program that offered. Please contact us know for best arrangement.

Places of interest will be visited during this tour

#1. Horse Riding

2 hours horse riding at Pererenan Village start at 8.30 and then explore Pererenan village such as rice field and Pererenan beach. Finish at 10.30 and then visit next destination.

#2. Tanah Lot Temple

Visit Tanah Lot temple which is set on the rock and one of popular destination in Bali especially at sunset time.

#3. Jatiluwih Rice Terrace

Next destination is visit Jatiluwih rice terrace located in Jatiluwih village. Jatiluwih rice terrace is one UNISCO heritage, on this place you will see beauty of rice terrace view.

#4. Ulundanu Temple

Popular tourist attraction located in Candikuning village, which offer beautiful of view set on lakeside of Beratan.

#5. Luwak Coffee Plantation

Visit Luwak Coffee Plantation is our last stop on this trip. Here you can see and taste many kind of coffee, one of coffee will be serving is Luwak Coffee. Luwak coffee is a seeds taken from civet dropping and then undergoes fermentation naturally then issued in the form of coffee beans and believed have different flavor that have higher quality than arabica coffee.
