Bali Water Rafting and Elephant Ride Tour

Bali water rafting and Elephant ride tour is an exciting Bali Combination tour we created to enjoy 2  hours water rafting and then continue 30 minutes Elephant Ride. The tour start at 08.00 am from your hotel and then our driver will take you to the starting point for Bali rafting activities and finish at 12.00 noon, then enjoy your lunch which is will be served after water rafting activities.

Then our driver will take you to Bakas Elepant safari Park located in Bakas village, Kelungkung regency. In here you will enjoy 30 minutes Elephant ride, see beautiful panoraman views surrounds the park from the teak chair on the top of the elephant.

Bali rafting and Elephant ride tour itinerary has been designed as well as possible, however we will be glad if you need combine with other activities or visit other popular places. Please have a look our tour itinerary as below and please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.

  • 12:00 – Lunch
  • 14:00 – Visit Bakas Elephant Safari Park
