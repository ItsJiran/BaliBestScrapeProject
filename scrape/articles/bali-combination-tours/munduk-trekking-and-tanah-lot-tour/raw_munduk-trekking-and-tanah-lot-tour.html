Munduk Trekking and Tanah Lot Tour

Munduk Trekking and Tanah Lot Tour is an exciting Bali combination tour packages we design for those of you who are interest to see Bali countryside in north part of Bali. The tour start at 08:00 am from your hotel stay in Bali and then our driver will take you Munduk Village. Arrive at starting point at 07:30 am and then our professional trekking guide will take to explore Munduk Village through trofical rainforest, rice terraces, and farms producing traditional commodities.

Then visit Luwak Coffee or Coffee plantation to see how to made coffee with traditional process, on this place you can taste Luwak coffee and others Bali coffee product. Then visit Tanah Lot temple located in Beraban Village, Tabanan Regency. Tanah Lot Temple is last tourist spot for this tour, this temple is popular tourist place in the island of Bali built on the rock and surrounded by the sea.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour

This tour itinerary has been designed based on our experience and our guest recommendation, however we can replace by visit other Bali sightseeing based on guest request if one of our tour itinerary has been visit.
