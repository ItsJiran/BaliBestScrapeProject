Tamblingan Trekking and Tanah lot Tour

Tamblingan Trekking and Tanah lot Tour is best way to discover the natural in north part of Bali island. The tour will take you to visit Tamblingan tropical rainforest which is one of best trekking spot in northern Bali with duration 2 hours trekking to explore Tamblingan forest and past the lake with traditional boat ride or canoe. And included visit Hindus temple in the forest which is a heritage of antiquity and still awake the beauty.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour
