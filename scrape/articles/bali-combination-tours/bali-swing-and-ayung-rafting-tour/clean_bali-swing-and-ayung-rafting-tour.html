Bali Swing and Ayung Rafting Tour

Bali Swing and Ayung Rafting Tour is great Bali combination tour package that we created perfectly and one of best tourist attraction that you have to do when in Bali island. Bali swing and rafting tour start at 8 am from your hotel and then our driver will take you to Ayung River for 2 hours rafting adventure. Finish at 12:00 for Rafting and then enjoy your lunch which is already included on this package and will serve after rafting adventure.

Then our tour guide will drive you to Luwak Coffee Plantations. On this place you will see how to make a coffee with with traditional proccess and you can try more than five type of coffee and the best coffee product namely Luwak Coffee which is popular with best quality, if you are coffee lover must try this. Then our driver will take you to Uma Pakel Swing located in Tegalalang Rice village, one of best place for swing activities in the island of Bali.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour

#1. Ayung Rafting

#2. Bali Swing

#3, Luwak Coffee Plantations
