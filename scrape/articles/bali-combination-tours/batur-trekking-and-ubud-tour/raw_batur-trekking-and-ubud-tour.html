Batur Trekking and Ubud Tour

Batur Trekking and Ubud Tour is one of an exciting Bali combination tour packages we created to visit the top of Mount Batur to see beautiful sun rises through Bali Trekking Activities. The tour start at 01.30 from your hotel and then our driver will take you to starting point. Will be arrive at 3:30 am in starting point and then having breakfast, before trekking begins.

Finish at 09.30 am for trekking activities and then our driver will take you visit coffee Luwak plantation. Here you can see and try various types of coffee, one of it is civet coffee. Civet coffee is the most expensive coffee in the world that have properties and unique taste.

Then we will visit Tegalalang Rice Terraces located in Tegalalang village, offer  beautiful natural scenery. After that we will having lunch at Local restaurant with rice terraces views. Then our driver will visit Ubud Palace located in the middle of Ubud village. Then visit Ubud Art Market which is the last tourist spot for this tours.

Interesting places will be visited during this tour

Batur Trekking and Ubud Tour itinerary has been designed based on our experience in the field and our guest recommendation, however we are more than happy to change it with other activities or other tourist place based on your request. Please have a look our tour itinerary as below and please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.

Tour Itinerary:

  • 01:30 – Pick up at your hotel ( Legian, Kuta, Seminyak and Jimbaran )
  • 03:00 – Arrive at starting point and having breakfast
  • 03:30 – Small briefing by professional trekking guide
  • 04:00 – Start for trekking to the crater summit
  • 06:00 – We arrive at the crater summit where we soak up the spectacular sunset and views
  • 07:00 – Back to finish point
  • 09:30 – Arrive at Finish point and then our driver will take you to visit Luwak Coffee plantation
  • 11:00 – Visit Tegalalang Rice terraces
  • 12:00 – Lunch at local restaurant
  • 14:00 – Visit Ubud Palace
  • 14:30 – Visit Ubud Art Market
  • 15:30 – Back to hotel
  • 17:00 – Arrive at Hotel
