Bali Swing and Uluwatu Temple Tour

Bali Swing and Uluwatu Temple Tour is one of the most popular Bali Combination Tours that you can do when on vacation in the island of Bali. This Tour will take you to the most popular Bali Swing Activities which is located in Bongkasa Village. Beside that this package will combine by visiting the most popular interesting places in the south part of Bali island.

#1. Visit Luwak Coffee Plantation

#2. Padang Padang Beach

#3. Visit Uluwatu Temple

Set on the top of cliff bank and one of six keys temples believed to be Balinese spiritual pillars. Offer best views from the top especially at sunset time.

#4. Watching Kecak and Fire Dance

Bali Swing and Uluwatu Tour has been designed based on our experience in tourist field and our guest recommendations, however you can change it with other activities or other interesting places based on your request. Please check our tour itinerary as follow and please feel free to contact us for best arrangement.

  • 08:00 – Pick up at your hotel

  • 18:00 – Watching Kecak and fire dance at Uluwatu
  • 19:00 – Back to Hotel
